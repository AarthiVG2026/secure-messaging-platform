# User Guide

Welcome to the Signal Clone! This guide explains how to use the core features of the application.

## 👤 Getting Started

### Register a New Account
1. Open the application and click **Create an Account**.
2. Enter your desired **Display Name**, **Phone Number**, and **Username**.
3. Set your password (e.g., `password123`).
4. On the next screen, enter the Mock Verification Code (OTP): **`123456`**.
5. You will be automatically logged in and redirected to the home screen.

### Login
1. On the login screen, enter your **Phone Number** or **Username** (e.g., `amit`).
2. Enter your password.
3. Enter the Mock OTP: **`123456`**.

---

## 📇 Contacts Management

By default, Signal does not let you chat with everyone on the platform. You must explicitly add them to your contacts first.

### Add a Contact
1. Click the **`+` (Plus icon)** at the top of the left sidebar.
2. Enter the exact **Phone Number** of the user you want to add (e.g., `+91 9000000001`).
3. *(Optional)* Add a custom Nickname.
4. Click **Search**. If the user exists, click **Add**.

### Search Contacts
You can search through your added contacts using the search bar at the top of the left sidebar. This searches both their display name and username.

---

## 💬 Messaging

### Create a Chat (1-on-1)
1. Click the **Pencil icon** (New Chat) located inside the search bar on the left sidebar.
2. Select a user from your contacts list.
3. A new conversation will instantly open. The conversation will appear in your sidebar permanently once a message is sent.

### Send a Message
1. Type your message in the input box at the bottom of the chat area.
2. Press **Enter** or click the send button.
3. The message will immediately appear in your chat history.

### Message Status Indicators
Next to the time on your sent messages, you will see tick marks indicating delivery status:
- 🕒 **Clock:** Sending...
- ✓ **Single Gray Tick:** Sent to server.
- ✓✓ **Double Gray Tick:** Delivered to recipient's device.
- ✓✓ **Double Blue Tick:** Read by recipient.

### Typing Indicator
When the other person is typing, a small animated typing bubble will appear above the message input field.

---

## 👥 Group Chats

### Create a Group
1. Click the **Users icon** at the top of the left sidebar.
2. Enter a **Group Name** and optional description.
3. Check the boxes next to the contacts you wish to add to the group.
4. Click **Create Group**.

### Manage Group (Admin)
If you created the group, or have been promoted to Admin:
1. Open the group chat.
2. Click on the Group Header to open the **Group Info Drawer** on the right side.
3. From here you can:
   - Click **Add Member** to add a new user by their exact username.
   - Click **Promote** next to a member to make them an Admin.
   - Click **Remove** to kick a user.

---

## ⚙️ Settings
1. Click the **Gear icon** at the top of the left sidebar.
2. Toggle the Application Theme between Light, Dark, or System.
3. Log out of the application using the **Log Out** button.

### Edit Profile
1. Click your **Avatar** at the top left of the screen.
2. Update your Display Name, Bio, or Profile Picture URL.
3. Click **Save**.
